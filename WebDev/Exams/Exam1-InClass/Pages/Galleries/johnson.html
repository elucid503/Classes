The Johnson Family Gallery

With many different ancestors, the Johnson family has a rich history. Two most notable members of the Johnson family are Greg and Melanie. They were born in the 1900s and lived in the United States. They and their children are still alive today.

Greg Johnson

Greg Johnson was born in 1964 and lives in the United States. As a hobby, Greg enjoys playing the guitar. He has a wife and two children.

Melanie Johnson

Melanie Johnson was born in 1966 and lives in the United States. Melanie enjoys playing the guitar and also singing. She has a husband and two children.
