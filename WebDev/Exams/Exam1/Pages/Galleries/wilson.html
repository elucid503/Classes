The Wilson Family Gallery

The Wilson is a much older family with an equally rich history. With multiple surnames and ancestors dating back to the 1800s, the Wilson family has a lot of stories to tell. Here are some of the Wilson family members.

Brian Wilson

Brian Wilson was born in 1966 and lives in the United Kingdom. He is a carpenter and has a wife and two children. Brian is still alive today. As a hobby, Brian enjoys working on cars.

Irene Wilson

Irene Wilson was born in 1915 and lived in the United Kingdom. She was a teacher and had a husband and one child. Irene passed away in 1991. Her daughter is still alive today.

Tim Wilson

Tim Wilson was born in 1920 and lived in the United Kingdom. He was a farmer and had a wife and three children. Tim passed away in 1990. His wife and children are still alive today.
