Chicken Stir-Fry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups water
  • 2 cups white rice
  • 2/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 3 skinless chicken breast halves
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup sliced bell peppers

Instructions:

  1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Stir in rice. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. In a small bowl, combine soy sauce, brown sugar, cornstarch, ginger, and garlic. Mix well.
  3. Cut chicken into 1/2 inch strips. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken and stir-fry until no longer pink and juices run clear, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add carrots, broccoli, and bell peppers. Stir-fry for 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in soy sauce mixture. Reduce heat and simmer until sauce thickens, about 5 minutes.
  6. Serve over rice.
